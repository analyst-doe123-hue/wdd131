BuildRight Construction

Website Planning Document

Site Name

BuildRight Construction & Engineering

This name was chosen because it clearly communicates the company's focus on quality construction ("BuildRight") while also indicating the professional engineering services offered. The name is memorable, professional, and inspires confidence in potential clients.

Optional domain availability: buildrightconstruction.com

Site Purpose

The BuildRight Construction website serves as a digital showcase of the company's capabilities, projects, and expertise in civil engineering and construction services. The site will:

  • Provide company information, mission, and values
  • Showcase completed and ongoing projects across residential, commercial, and infrastructure categories
  • Offer a convenient way for potential clients to contact the company
  • Establish credibility through testimonials and references
  • Demonstrate technical expertise and industry experience

Scenarios

These are questions that potential clients might ask when visiting the website:

  • Scenario 1: "I'm planning to build a commercial office building. Has this company completed similar projects, and can I see examples of their work?"
  • Scenario 2: "I need to contact someone about a potential infrastructure project. What's the best way to reach the appropriate department or project manager?"
  • Scenario 3: "How experienced is this company with residential construction, and what types of homes have they built?"
  • Scenario 4: "Can I see testimonials from previous clients to gauge their satisfaction with the company's work?"

Typography

The selected fonts prioritize readability while maintaining a professional appearance:

Arial (Headings)

This clean, sans-serif font will be used for all headings and navigation elements to create a strong, professional impression.

Sample: BuildRight Construction - Quality Since 2005

Segoe UI (Body Text)

This highly readable sans-serif font will be used for all body text, paragraphs, and general content to ensure optimal readability across devices.

Sample: Our company specializes in residential, commercial, and infrastructure projects with over 15 years of experience in the construction industry.

Technical Features

The website will implement the following technical features:

  • Responsive Design: Optimized for all device sizes using CSS media queries
  • Project Filtering: JavaScript-powered filtering system for project categories
  • Image Optimization: Compressed and properly sized images for fast loading
  • Form Handling: Contact form with client-side validation and localStorage backup
  • Dynamic Content: JavaScript for interactive elements like testimonials carousel
  • Accessibility: WCAG 2.1 AA compliance with proper semantic HTML and ARIA labels
